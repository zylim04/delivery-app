What is RMSE?

Root Mean Square Error — measures average prediction error in minutes. Lower is better.

What is R²?

R-squared — measures how much variance the model explains. Higher is better (max 1.0).

0.8393 means the model explains 83.93% of delivery time variation.

What is MAE?

Mean Absolute Error — average absolute difference between predicted and actual. Lower is better.
