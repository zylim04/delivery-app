Top Features Affecting Delivery Time
🔍 Key Findings
  • distance_km is the strongest predictor — longer distances mean longer delivery.
  • delivery_person_ratings — higher rated riders tend to deliver faster.
  • prep_time_min — restaurant preparation time directly adds to total time.
  • multiple_deliveries — riders handling multiple orders take longer.
  • road_traffic_density — Jam and High traffic significantly increase delivery time.

Feature importance was computed using a combination of Random Forest importance scores and Mutual Information (hybrid selection).
