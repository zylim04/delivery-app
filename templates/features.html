About SHAP

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) is a game-theory based method for explaining ML predictions.

Unlike basic feature importance, SHAP accounts for feature interactions and gives consistent, mathematically rigorous explanations.

Values shown are mean absolute SHAP values computed on 500 training samples.
